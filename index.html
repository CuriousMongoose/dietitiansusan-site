Meet Susan

A dietitian, a mom, and a woman who gets it.

I'm Susan — a registered dietitian with 13 years of clinical experience, a homeschooling mom, and someone who has seen firsthand how diet culture steals the joy right out of mealtimes.

For years I watched women walk into my consulting room carrying the same weight — not on their bodies, but on their hearts. Guilt. Shame. The quiet belief that if they could just try harder, eat less, be more disciplined, everything would finally be okay.

But that's not what I saw clinically. What I saw was biology being blamed on character. I saw mothers pouring everything into their families while running on empty themselves. And I saw that the real healing — the lasting kind — doesn't start with a meal plan. It starts with remembering who you were made to be.

That's what I help women do now. Not through another set of rules, but through grace, community, and the kind of practical guidance that actually fits into a real mother's life.

My Approach

Grace, not guilt. Adding, not subtracting.

Everything I teach is grounded in clinical evidence and rooted in Biblical truth. Here's what makes this different from every diet you've tried before.

Add, Don't Subtract

Instead of cutting out the foods you love, we focus on adding in the nourishment your body is actually asking for. No food is "off-limits." Your body is not the enemy.

Pause & Pray

Before you open the fridge, pause. Are you Hungry, Anxious, Lonely, or Tired? This simple clinical tool — the HALT method — helps you respond to what your heart really needs.

Three-Tier Goals

Minimum. Ideal. Outrageous. Instead of all-or-nothing perfectionism, you set three levels for every goal — so even on the hardest days, you're still moving forward.

Biblical Stewardship

Your body is not a project to fix — it's a gift to steward. We ground every step in the truth that God made food to be enjoyed and your body to be honoured.
